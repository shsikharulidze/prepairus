Frequently Asked Questions

Why are projects unpaid?

Projects are educational experiences where students are the primary beneficiaries. This framework ensures compliance with educational guidelines and makes the program accessible to international students.

How long are projects?

Most projects run 2-8 weeks and require 5-15 hours per week, designed to fit around academic schedules while providing substantial experience.

What kind of support do students get?

Students receive mentorship from business owners, project guidance, and portfolio development support. Each project includes clear learning objectives.

How are students and projects matched?

We match based on academic background, skills, interests, location preferences, and project requirements to ensure good fits for both parties.

What if a project doesn't work out?

We provide support to resolve issues and can facilitate project transitions when necessary. Clear expectations and communication prevent most problems.

Can students work on multiple projects?

Yes, students can take on multiple projects over time, but we recommend focusing on one project at a time for the best learning experience.
